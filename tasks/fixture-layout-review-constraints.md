# Fixture Layout Review Constraints

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-24 23:26 EDT

**Executive Summary:** Persona review for issue #59 is restricted to appearance, layout, structure, scanability, hierarchy, pagination, and artifact usability. Reviewers must not critique historical content quality, role fit, truthfulness, or whether the Einstein fixture is semantically appropriate for the engineering job fixture.

## In Scope

1. Visual hierarchy and page composition.
2. Pagination, whitespace balance, and density.
3. Section ordering and structural coherence.
4. ATS-facing structure and artifact legibility.
5. Whether the normalized review bundle is sufficient for manual review.

## Out Of Scope

1. Whether the candidate content is persuasive for the target role.
2. Whether the achievements, publications, or summary should be rewritten for better semantic fit.
3. Whether the engineering job fixture is the right job target.
4. Any proposal that writes back to master CV data.

## Rejection Rules

Reject or ignore findings when they primarily argue that:

1. The Einstein content should be replaced with more platform-engineering experience.
2. The summary should be rewritten to match job keywords better.
3. A different candidate, profile, or job fixture would produce more relevant content.
4. The review should expand into product-runtime persona critique instead of generated-material review.

## Required Evidence

Each persona review should cite the same run artifacts when possible:

1. `/tmp/issue59-phase3-bundle/normalized/render.png`
2. `/tmp/issue59-phase3-bundle/normalized/structure-outline.txt`
3. `/tmp/issue59-phase3-bundle/normalized/plain-text.txt`
4. `/tmp/issue59-phase3-bundle/fixture-review-manifest.json`
